What is Campus Ride?

Campus Ride is a mobile application that allows students to request rides to places on campus.

Students are encouraged to give rides to others, which will earn them Ride Points® that they can use to request rides for themselves.

This will allow students to get exactly where they want to go from anywhere on campus.
