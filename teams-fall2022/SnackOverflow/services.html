Yelp Roulette will randomly select a restraurant near you based on the settings you have chosen.

Filters

Provide an address to set a certain area that will make visiting generated restaurants convenient. Further narrow the search by selecting a price range, if the restaurant is currently open, and more.

Restaurant Information

Once a restaurant has been selected, brief yet important information about it will be displayed, such as open/closing times, price ranges, and an image of what to expect. If you need more information, a link to the restaurant's Yelp page is provided.

Favorites

Favorite restaurants that stand out to you; such restaurants will be saved in a list that you can reference at any time in the Favorites page. In addition, view past restaurants that you have generated so far.

User Accounts

Create an account to save your favorite and recently viewed restaurants for future reference.
