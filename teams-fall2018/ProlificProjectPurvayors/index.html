Prolific Project Purvayors

Bronco Events is a tool used by students to find when clubs are meeting. The good part is: everything is automatic. No need to sign up.

About

Bronco Events was created by Camron Fortenbery, (Richard) Matt Musquiz, Justin (Yeram) Hwang and Diego Bautista to make it easier for people to find what events are happening around campus without needing to sign up for E-mail newsletters. All events are easily searchable and information is displayed in a simple manner.
