Hearthstone Deck Builder

Maximize your potential.

A clean simplistic look at what players will want. This is a simple way for players to click on the class of the deck they want to use.

An easy dropdown menu for classes with multiple decks. User can choose a deck and it will autopopulate the deck page.

The total dust cost is displayed. The optimal pack for the cards needed is displayed.
