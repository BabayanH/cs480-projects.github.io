Solving the troubles of Parking at Cal Poly Pomona

Advertise your Parking Space

With this feature, one could advertise the parking spot that he or she parks in. You could then trade your parking spot with ariving riders who are desperately in need for a parking spot. You could provide a trade, which is a free ride to your parking spot so you won't have to walk for a long time for your prized spot. This create a much greater flow of traffic.

Look at available parking spots

As the driver, you can see the available times people want to get picked up. If you find it fitting all you have to do is click on one of the parking icons. This will set you up with the whole list of people who are registered based on that specific parking lot. After this you could then click on any of the persons who are being displayed, this will open your email client and you can begin to commmunicate.
